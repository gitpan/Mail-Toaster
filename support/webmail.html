What is Webmail?

Webmail applications are those to interface with standard mail boxes and present email to the reader via a web browser rather than a dedicated mail client.


Using Webmail?

We provide you with several webmail interfaces. Squirrelmail and Sqwebmail are both available from the Mail Center Home and both are very good web mail clients. We have also provded qmailadmin so that you can make adminsitrative changes to your mail settings such as enabling spam filtering, email forwarding, or vacation notices.
