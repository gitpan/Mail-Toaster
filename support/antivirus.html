What is Virus Scanning?

Virus Scanning is the process of scanning email messages and comparing any mail message attachments against a database of know virus signatures. We block and quarrantine any message that is infected with a virus or matches a common windows file extension that is typically associated with virus transmission.


Using Virus Scanning?

Virus filtering is enabled system wide on our mail system so your mail is automatically protected.
